MVMNT

MVMNT is a youth center dedicated to the community of North Miami Beach. Inspired by the program and existing skatepark , the form and circulation of the massing evoke a continual movement and sense of activity and place. One building emphasizes the center's entrepreneurship functions, while the other represents sports and activity. Using concrete from the original skatepark, the new skatepark on the site provides a visual point to onlookers from Collins Avenue. The wooden louvers minimize sunlight and solar gain while creating a "moving" facade as one walks or drives past.
