The Bass Innovation Hub

The Bass Museum, located in the heart of Miami Beach, is currently in the process of designing for its second addition. In this design, the addition imagines an Innnovation Hub, which exposes the process of art making and includes multipurpose spaces for events and coworking. The loop effect in its circulation combats the original museum's issue of wayfinding and enhances the visitor experience. Clearer wayfinding and visual cues through a colorful stair ensure a more cohesive , comfortable, and inviting museum space. The circulation is pushed out in the facade to further invite pedestrians and onlookers.
